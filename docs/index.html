Classical platformer

A small Platformer made in 12 hours for Ludum Dare 37. By Plab & Nid & Tbox

12h for a game

This game was made as in 12h by a team of three. With GameMaker and to practive more. Part of the challenge was to craft all the assets and all ready for HTML5 compatibility.

Controls

With a keyboard, use left and right arrows to run in that direction and space to jump. Use F11 or the F key to toggle fullscreen.

If for some reason to get stuck, it is possible to restart the current level by pressing the R key.

With a gamepad, use the thumb-stick or the d-pad in place of the keyboard arrows, the button A to jump.

Download

There is a Window binary available, and the HTML5 version. If your platform is not available, consider building it from sources.

Tools of the trade

We use Game Maker to build the game. Graphics are made with After effects and Photoshop. For sound we use Reaper, Fl Studio and we use Google Analytics to know if people play this game.
